Featured Book

of the Month

Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert T. Kiyosaki

Rich Dad Poor Dad explores the financial lessons Robert Kiyosaki learned from two father figures: his biological father (Poor Dad) and his best friend's father (Rich Dad). Through contrasting perspectives, Kiyosaki highlights the importance of financial education, investing, and entrepreneurial thinking. The book advocates for building wealth through real estate and business investments rather than traditional employment and savings. Kiyosaki emphasizes that financial independence is achievable through learning, strategic investing, and understanding money management principles.

Our Special

Combos

Espresso Crave

& Cake

Espresso Crave & Cake offers a delightful fusion of rich, aromatic espresso paired with decadent cake delicacies, creating a perfect balance between bold flavors and sweet indulgence. Each dish is crafted to elevate your coffee experience, with cakes that range from velvety chocolate to delicate fruit tarts. This culinary journey complements the ethos of "Attitude is Everything Book"—a book-themed café where every page you turn enhances your coffee experience. The cozy, literary atmosphere invites guests to savor their treats while diving into stories, making it an ideal spot for readers and coffee lovers to relax and enjoy.

Donut

& Espresso

Donut & Espresso Combo offers a delightful pairing of a freshly made donut and a bold, rich espresso shot. The contrast of the donut’s soft sweetness with the espresso’s robust bitterness creates a satisfying taste experience. Perfect for a quick break, this combo energizes both your body and mind. Pairing this treat with the "Psychology of Money" book adds a thoughtful twist. As you enjoy the flavors, dive into the book's insights on the emotional and behavioral aspects of finance, making it a reflective and energizing experience—food for both thought and appetite.
